Kenneth Andrews is a martial artist, and amateur fighter from mid michigan. He started training and competing in Tang Soo Do as a teenager. He competed in many karate tournaments and took 3 first place trophies at the 2008 National All Martial Arts Championship in Pittsburgh. He eventually got involved in mixed martial arts winning his first fight at the age of 16.
